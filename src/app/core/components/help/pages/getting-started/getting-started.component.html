Getting started

To access the dashboard you will first need to create an account. You can do this by clicking “Register an account” on the authentication page, or simply by visiting the direct URL: {{getSignUpUrl()}}.

You will be asked to either provide your own key or generate a new key pair on premise.

In case you choose to generate the keys on the website, make sure to download the file containing it, confirm and proceed.

As a final step, you will be asked to provide your organization name (optional), e-mail and reason for joining.

Once submitted, your account is subject to manual approval. Once reviewed by our administrator, you will receive a notification e-mail with further instructions.
