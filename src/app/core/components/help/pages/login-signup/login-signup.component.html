Login

There are two options for login:

  • Login with private key
  • Login with email

In both cases, you have to have an account (public key/address registered in AMB-NET).

After getting an account, on first login, you have to use your private key, then go to settings and set the password. After that, you can login with your email and password.

Signup

There are two ways to get an account:

  • Get invited by an organizational owner/admin, to join their organization in AMB-NET
  • Make a request for an organization

Organization request

If you are on login page, click on 'Register an account', and follow the steps.

You can choose between registering your own public key/address or generate new keys.

Then, fill in the form. Organization title, your email and a message.

On successful submit, once whether your organization request gets approved or declined, you will be notified by email.

On organization request approval, your account will been approved, along side your organization. You can login, start making assets, events, and even invite other people to join your organization.
